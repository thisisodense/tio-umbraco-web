Tag Manager

Welcome to your Tag Manager dashboard where you are able to:

  • rename tags to fix spelling errors, etc
  • merge tags due to spelling errors or to group together similiar words / tags
  • delete tags

To get started simply expand the tag group(s) on the left and click on a tag to edit.
